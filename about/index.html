What We Do

A marketplace designed for builders, not brokers

Siterifty is a peer-to-peer marketplace for buying and selling websites, SaaS products, newsletters, eCommerce stores, and online businesses of all sizes. We've stripped out the noise — no bloated broker fees, no endless back-and-forth — just a clean, fast platform that connects serious buyers with motivated sellers.

Whether you're flipping a side project for a few hundred dollars or exiting a six-figure SaaS, Siterifty gives you the tools, escrow protection, and audience you need to close confidently.

Our Journey

From idea to the #1 indie marketplace

We started Siterifty because we were tired of the same problems every founder faces when trying to exit or acquire digital assets. Here's how we got here.

2022 — The Frustration
An idea born from a bad experience

Our founders tried to sell a bootstrapped SaaS. Every broker wanted 10–20% and a 6-month exclusive. We said no — and decided to build something better.

2023 — First Launch
Beta opens to 200 indie founders

We launched a private beta, onboarded our first 200 sellers, and closed our first 50 deals within 3 months. Word spread fast in the indie community.

2024 — Escrow & Trust
Built-in escrow for every transaction

We launched our native escrow system, eliminating the biggest risk in peer-to-peer website sales. Deal volume grew 400% in 6 months.

2025 — Scale
10,000+ buyers, $1M+ in deals closed

Siterifty became the go-to marketplace for indie founders and digital entrepreneurs worldwide. We're just getting started.
