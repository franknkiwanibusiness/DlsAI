Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does the battery last on a single charge?

On Eco Mode you get up to 16 hours of continuous use. Normal Mode gives around 8 hours and Turbo Mode around 3 hours. The 4000mAh battery charges fully in under 2 hours via USB-C.

Is the fan loud? Can I use it in meetings or at my desk?

The brushless motor runs almost silently on lower speeds. At speed 1–40 it's barely audible. The 100-level precision control means you can set it to exactly what you need without disturbing others.

What is the ice-cold plate and how does it work?

The aerospace alloy ice plate on the fan face drops in temperature as the motor runs, delivering cooled airflow rather than just moving room-temperature air. It's a passive cooling tech — no ice needed, no refills.

How long does shipping take and can I track my order?

Orders are processed within 24 hours and delivered in 7–14 business days depending on your location. A tracking link is sent to your email once dispatched. Bundle orders ship free.

Can I use it while it's charging?

Yes. The USB-C port supports pass-through charging, so you can plug in and keep cooling without interruption. Ideal for desk or bedside use when you don't want to wait for a full charge.

What's the difference between the colour variants?

All four colours — Phantom Black, Arctic White, Ice Blue, and Sakura Pink — are identical in performance and specs. The only difference is the shell colour. Sakura Pink is a limited run so stock is lower.

Is there a warranty and what does it cover?

Every FrostBlade Pro comes with a 12-month warranty covering manufacturing defects, motor failure, and battery issues. Just contact support with your order ID and we'll sort it out — replacement or full refund.

Can I buy one as a gift? Does it come in gift packaging?

Absolutely. The FrostBlade Pro ships in a premium retail box that's gift-ready straight out of the bag. The Dual Pack and Family Pack are popular gift options — and they ship free too.
