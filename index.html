Care & Maintenance

Keep your MINIMISTY™ in peak condition with these simple care tips:

  • Wipe exterior with a slightly damp cloth. Do not submerge.
  • Clean fan blades monthly with a soft brush.
  • For ice compress mode, refill the water reservoir with clean water only.
  • Store at room temperature. Avoid direct sunlight for long periods.
  • Charge with a USB-C cable rated at 5V/2A or higher.
  • Do not use with third-party fast-chargers above 18W.
