Rule Book

01. Identity Lock

Managers must use the same Team Name and Logo throughout the season. Changing identity mid-season results in a -10 PTS penalty.

02. The 15-Minute Rule

Matches must kick off within 15 minutes of the scheduled time. If your opponent is 5 mins late, report for a 3-0 Walkover.

03. Network Integrity

In case of a 'Connection Lost' error, players must play a rematch starting from the scoreline at the time of disconnect.

04. Proof of Play

Winning managers must upload a screenshot of the 'Match Stats' screen to the League Lobby within 30 mins of full-time.

05. Roster Caps

No duplicate legends are allowed if restricted by the season admin. Max squad size for League 1 is 32 players.

06. Zero-Tolerance Cheating

Using external hacks, lag switches, or APK mods results in an immediate permanent ban and data wipe.

07. Lobby Conduct

Toxic behavior, spamming, or harassment in the chat window leads to a 3-match suspension from league play.

08. Safe Zone Protocol

The bottom 3 managers at the end of the season are automatically replaced by waiting-list managers.

09. High-Stake Video

For UCL Finals and Promotion playoffs, screen recording is mandatory to settle disputes.

10. Admin Sovereignty

The League Admin has the final say on all match disputes. Rules are subject to update before each New Season.
