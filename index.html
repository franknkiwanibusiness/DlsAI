Common Questions
How do I list a site for sale?
Go to the main marketplace and tap + List a Site. Fill in the details — URL, revenue, traffic, asking price — and submit. Your listing goes live immediately on the Free plan (1 slot).
What payment methods are accepted?
We currently process payments through PayPal. All purchases and subscription payments are handled securely. Your wallet balance can be used toward any purchase.
What are the platform fees?
Free: 30%. Starter: 15%. Growth: 10%. Pro: 5% — the lowest. Upgrade anytime from the plan section above.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, anytime. Switch to the Free plan from the plan picker above. Your paid features remain active until the end of the current billing period. No penalties.
How does wallet balance work?
Top up your wallet via PayPal. Balance covers site purchases, plan payments, and hosting fees — all in one place. Balances never expire and are non-refundable store credit.
