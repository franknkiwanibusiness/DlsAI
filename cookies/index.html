Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  Effective immediately

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how you use Siterifty so we can improve your experience.

This policy explains the types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

Cookies We Use

The table below outlines the cookies currently used on Siterifty and their purpose.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
session_id Essential Keeps you signed in during your session Session
auth_token Essential Authenticates your account securely 30 days
theme_pref Functional Remembers your display preferences 1 year
_analytics Analytics Aggregated usage stats to improve the platform 90 days
pp_session Essential PayPal payment flow session Session
Cookie Categories

Essential Cookies — Required for the marketplace to function. These cannot be disabled. They handle authentication, security, and payment sessions.

Functional Cookies — Remember your preferences such as display settings, filters, and saved searches. Disabling these may affect your experience.

Analytics Cookies — Help us understand aggregate traffic patterns so we can improve listings, performance, and features. No personally identifiable data is sold.

Third-Party Cookies

Siterifty integrates with third-party services that may set their own cookies on your device:

  • PayPal — Sets cookies during checkout to secure payment sessions.
  • Firebase (Google) — Used for authentication. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Imgur — Used for profile image hosting when you upload an avatar.

We do not control third-party cookies. Please review each provider's own cookie and privacy policies for details.

Managing Your Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Common options:

  • Block all cookies — Most browsers let you block cookies entirely. Note that essential features like sign-in will not work.
  • Delete existing cookies — You can clear cookies stored in your browser at any time via browser settings.
  • Opt-out of analytics — You can opt out of aggregated analytics by emailing us at privacy@siterifty.com.

Browser-specific instructions are available at allaboutcookies.org.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our services. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Siterifty after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
