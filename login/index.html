01. Payment Guide

We use MiniPay for lightning-fast, secure transactions.

  1. Open your MiniPay wallet link from the dashboard.
  2. Transfer the entry fee to our verified tournament address.
  3. Copy your transaction ID/Hash.
  4. Paste the ID into the Master Command portal for instant verification.

Check our tutorial video on the dashboard if you encounter any issues.
