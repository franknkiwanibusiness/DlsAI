Anti-Cheat Protocol

  • ●

    No Rage Quitting: If you leave before the 90th minute while losing, you lose the tie 5-0 automatically.

  • ●

    Connection Lag: If the game is unplayable due to lag, stop at minute 10 and record it. Playing past 20 mins means you accept the conditions.

  • ●

    Rating Caps: Players caught using a 100-rated squad when the limit is lower will be disqualified instantly.

  • ●

    Score Proof: No Screenshot = No Result. Both managers must post the score in the WhatsApp group within 10 mins of the match ending.

Match Procedure

1

2

R16, QF, and SF are Two-Legged. Away goals do NOT count. If tied, play a 3rd match (Golden Goal).

3

The Budapest Final is a single 90-minute match. Extra time/Pens allowed.
