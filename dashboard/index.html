Questions
What can my wallet balance be used for?
Your balance can be used for premium purchases, plan upgrades (Duo, Max), limited-edition drops, add-ons, and any future credits or promotions we offer. It never expires.
Can I withdraw my balance?
Wallet balances are non-refundable and cannot be withdrawn to a bank or PayPal account. The balance is store credit only. If you believe there's been an error, contact support.
Why is there a processing fee?
PayPal charges a standard processing fee of 3.49% + $0.49 for each transaction. This fee goes directly to PayPal — Siterifty does not keep any of it.
Is this payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed by PayPal using 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details. Your payment is covered by PayPal Buyer Protection.
Does my balance expire?
No. Your wallet balance never expires. You can top up whenever you like and spend at your own pace with no time pressure.
